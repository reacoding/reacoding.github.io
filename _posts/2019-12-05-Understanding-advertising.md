----------------------
### Phrases
When giving additional information or contrasting information, we often connect the ideas by using phrases such as these. Using '**pauses**' to connect.
- despite the fact that
- in spite of
- although
- even though
- instead of
- not only ... but also ...
- on the other hand
- in addition
- additionally
- moreover
- consequently
- therefore
- as a result
- due to
